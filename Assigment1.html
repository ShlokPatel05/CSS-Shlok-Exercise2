Grilled Cheese Sandwiches with Bacon and Tomato

Ingredients:

  • Bacon
  • Cheese slices
  • Tomato slices
  • Butter
  • Bread slices

Instructions:

1. Preheat a skillet or griddle over medium heat.

2. Place bacon slices on the skillet and cook until crispy. Remove and drain on paper towels.

3. Butter one side of each bread slice.

4. Place one slice of bread, butter side down, on the skillet.

5. Layer cheese, bacon, tomato slices, and more cheese on top of the bread.

6. Top with another slice of bread, butter side up.

7. Cook until the bread is golden brown and the cheese is melted, flipping halfway through.

8. Remove from heat, slice if desired, and serve hot.
